Project

SAC + Flow-Matching Actions

2024 · Robotics RL

A soft actor-critic policy paired with a flow-matched action generator to smooth trajectories and reduce jitter on hardware.

FocusControl smoothness
StackPython, PyTorch
ArtifactsCheckpoints, rollout videos

Overview

Coupled SAC with a flow-matching model that proposes action trajectories respecting hardware limits. The flow model shapes proposals; SAC handles reward-driven selection.

Highlights

  • Flow proposals trained on smoothed demonstrations to set a baseline trajectory prior.
  • Safety envelope baked into proposals to avoid saturation and jerk.
  • Closed-loop evaluation with rollouts saved for offline review.
Drop rollout videos, jerk/acceleration plots, and architecture diagrams here.

Notes

Reward shaping focused on smooth accelerations and task success. Flow proposals reduced SAC action noise without hurting convergence.

Artifacts

Policy checkpoints, flow model weights, and evaluation scripts are organized for hardware tests or further tuning.
